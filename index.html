What to Bring for Your Passport Application

For Adult First-Time Applicants

  • Proof of U.S. Citizenship: Certified birth certificate or naturalization certificate (original)
  • Photo ID: Valid driver's license, government ID, or military ID
  • Passport Photo: One 2x2 inch color photo (available on-site for $15)
  • Form DS-11: Completed but unsigned (sign at facility)
  • Payment: $130 check/money order to "U.S. Department of State" + $35 execution fee (cash, check, or card)

For Child Passport (Under 16)

  • Child's Birth Certificate: Original or certified copy
  • Both Parents Present: Both parents must appear with child
  • Parents' Photo IDs: Valid government-issued IDs for both parents
  • Child's Photo: One 2x2 inch passport photo of child
  • Payment: $100 check/money order + $35 execution fee

Important: Do Not Sign Form DS-11

You must sign your passport application Form DS-11 in front of the acceptance agent at the facility. If you sign it beforehand, your application will be rejected and you'll need to fill out a new form.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment at this passport facility?

Recommended but walk-ins accepted. While walk-ins are typically accepted during passport service hours, calling ahead to schedule an appointment is strongly recommended to avoid long wait times, especially during peak travel seasons (March-August).

Can I get passport photos at this location?

Yes, this facility offers on-site passport photo services for $15. Photos meet all U.S. Department of State requirements (2x2 inches, white background, taken within 6 months). This allows you to complete your entire application in one visit.

What forms of payment are accepted?

The $35 execution fee can be paid by cash, check, money order, or major credit/debit cards. The passport application fee ($130 for book, $30 for card) must be paid by check or money order made payable to "U.S. Department of State."

How long does it take to process my passport?

Routine processing takes 4-6 weeks, while expedited service (additional $60 fee) takes 2-3 weeks. These times are from when the State Department receives your application, plus mailing time. For urgent travel within 14 days, visit a Regional Passport Agency.

Can I renew my passport at this facility?

No, passport renewals cannot be done at acceptance facilities. If you're eligible to renew (passport issued when 16+, within last 15 years, undamaged, same name), you must renew by mail using Form DS-82 or online through the State Department's renewal system.

Is this facility wheelchair accessible?

Yes, this location is fully ADA compliant with wheelchair accessible entrances, service counters, and restroom facilities. If you need special assistance, mention it when calling to schedule your appointment.
